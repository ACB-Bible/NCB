New Christian Bible - About

The New Christian Bible is part of the Bible Code Project which is an ongoing Bible development project with multiple goals. Some of these goals include a Bible website for Bible study and Sunday services, and it can be used with many different devices. The Bible Code Project contains source code and data files that are in the public domain, and none of it is copyrighted. It's free to use for churches, organizations, and individuals in their services, bible study groups, or even their own own bible projects for free. God is not a business and the bible is not a product, so all of this is free for you to use, and it's not for sale.



The Bible Code Project

Bible Code Project Documentation for Developers
If you'd like to try your hand at developing your own bible website, the Bible Code Project is a collection of GitHub repositories containing source code and data that anyone can use to develop their own computerized bible project. New Christian Bible - Statement of Faith

These are The Things I Believe

I. I believe there is only one God, who is, was, and always will be, and I believe that God is the Holy Trinity: Father, Son and the Holy Spirit.

II. I believe that Jesus said, 'If you have seen me you have seen the Father.'

III. I believe that Jesus Christ was immacuately conceived by the Holy Spirit and was born to a virgin named Mary in a little town named Bethlehem.

IV. I believe that Jesus came to earth not to judge the world, but to save it.

V. I believe that Jesus is the Way, the Truth, and the Life.

VI. I believe that Jesus came to fulfill all things written, and the kingdom of God is at hand

VII. I believe we must repent of our sins and believe in the gospel.

VIII. I believe that the Kingdom of Heaven is here, now because I have seen it.

IX. I believe that Jesus Christ lived, was crucified, died, and rose again on the third day after he was entombed to save us from our sin.
