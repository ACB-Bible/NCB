List of Movies

  1. Joshua
    2002 • 1 hr 30 min • G • Drama
    A kind unknown carpenter arrives in the rural town of Auburn and begins repairing a small Baptist church that was destroyed in a storm the year before. When they see him performing miracles, the whole town is shaken to it's core when they realize that he may be the risen Christ.

  2. Bible Collection Movies
    A series of movies based on the stories in the Bible.

    1. The Bible Collection - Jesus
      1989 • TV-PG • 2h 54m • Drama
      A young carpenter, Jesus of Nazerath, embarks on a journey to fulfill his destiny as the savior of the world.

    2. The Bible Collection - Solomon
      2020 • 1h 50m • TV-14 • Drama
      Israel's King Solomon falls in love with the beautiful Queen of Sheba, but she must return to her own country. When she leaves, he falls into a great depression which threatens his own kingdom.

    3. The Bible Collection - The Apocalypse
      2015 • 1h 36m • TV-PG • Drama
      The BIble tells of John's visions during a time of severe persecution for Christians that led to major revelations about God's plans.

  3. The Encounter
    2010 • TV-PG • 1h 30m • Drama • Inspirational Fiction
    After a road is closed, five strangers find themselves involuntarily introduced to each other at an abandoned roadside diner where they experience a supernatural encounter with Jesus Christ.

  4. The Encounter - Paradise Lost
    2012 • 1 hr 49 min • TV-14 • Drama • Inspirational Fiction
    Six strangers find themselves facing a hurricane as they are trapped in a beach hotel, seven years after the most devastating tsunami in Thailand.

  5. The Life of Jesus
    2003 • PG-13 • 3h 3m • Drama
    A re-enactment of the life of Jesus as testified to in the Gospels by John the Apostle.

List of Seasonal Shows

  1. A.D. The Bible Continues
    2015 • TV-14 • 1 Season • Drama
    A TV miniseries about Jesus Christ's disciples, his mother Mary, and other political or religious personalities after his crucifixion.

  2. The Bible
    2013 • PG-14 • 1 Season • Drama
    The Bible TV miniseries is about God's creation of the universe and the events leading up to the crucifixion of His son Jesus Christ, and his resurrection.

  3. The Chosen
    2017 • TVPG • 1 Season • Drama
    The Chosen is a multi-season miniseries about the life of Jesus Christ. The series brings you a deeper understanding of Jesus Christ and the people who knew Him from the four gospels in the Bible.

  4. The Encounter
    2016 • TV-PG • 1 Season • Drama • Inspirational Fiction
    This series of shows presents the lives of many people, who are faced with different obstacles in their life, when Jesus steps in to help them overcome their problems and restore their faith.
