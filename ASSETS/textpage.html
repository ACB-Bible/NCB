New Christian Bible - Ministries

If you've ever been involved in spreading the the Good News and teachings of Jesus Christ, then you know well the suffering your soul has had to endure from the lies and attacks thrown at you from darkness that many of his followers have suffered from the beginning. Many of God's ministries today are on the TV, internet, radio, and in many other types of electronic media. These are some of the ones that I've read, watched, or listened to over the years. They've strengthened my faith, brought new light into the darkness, and I've learned a lot from them. Every time my soul gets tired and I feel my faith dragging, I turn to one of these ministers, and they heal my spirit and give rest to my soul. I really like these ministries, and I beleive that they're truly doing God's work. Listen to them and they'll help fill you with the Holy Spirit.


Ministries

  1. Arnold Murray • Stream
    (1927 - 2014)
    Website -•-> Shepherds Chapel
    I began watching Pastor Dr. Arnold Murray about ten years ago. I watched him for many years, and I didn't know until today that he'd passed away years before I began watching his Sunday morning services, I was sorry to hear this news because I always learned something from him when he spoke. I find his sermons to be very educational and filled with the Holy Spirit. I still enjoy watching and listening to his sermons, his message is always honest and uplifting, and I think you'll like him too.

  2. Charles Stanley • Stream
    (1932 - 2023)
    Website -•-> InTouch Ministries
    Dr. Charles Stanley has been my pastor since I first discovered his ministry in the early 1980's. I was very saddened to hear of his death, he was a good shepherd, and I'm going to miss him. I've been watching his Sunday morning TV show most Sunday's since the 80's, and I still watch his rerun sermons to this day: I think you'll like him too.

  3. David Jeremiah • Stream
    Website -•-> Turning Point Ministries
    Dr. David Jeremiah believes our dreams should be greater than our memories, and we should seize every opportunity God puts in our path, which allows us to continue growing and reach new hearts with God's Word. I've been watching Dr. Jeremiah since the 1980's, his sermons always lift my spirits, and I think you'll like him too.

  4. K-Love • Stream Live
    Website -•-> K-love Radio
    I just recently began listening to K-Love Radio and I really enjoy it. The DJ's are bright and cheerful, and God's word comes through strong and powerfully. I listen to this broadcast every time I'm in my car going anywhere. Give it a try and maybe you'll like it too.

  5. John Ancherberg • Stream
    Website -•-> John Ancherberg Show
    I've been watching Dr. John Ancherberg a long time, probably about since the early 1980's. John's teachings are inspirational, spirit filled, and very educational. I've learned a lot from Dr. Ancherberg, and I think you can learn a lot from him too.

  6. Joyce Meyer • Stream
    (1927 - 2023)
    Website -•-> Joyce Meyer Ministries
    I don't know how long I've been watching Joyce Meyer, but it's been a long time. She always has something inspirational and spirit filled to teach, and I enjoy listening to her. Joyce brings us an uplifting message from God, and I think you can learn a lot from her.

  7. Les Feldick • Stream
    (1927 - 2023)
    Website -•-> Through the Bible with Les Feldick
    I've been watching Through the Bible with Les Feldick for many years and I find his sermons to be very educational and spirit filled. I enjoy watching and listening to his sermons, his message was always spirit filled. I could always feel the Holy Spirit through the teachings of Les Feldick's sermons, and I think you'll like him too. It made me very sad to learn of his passing.



The Bible Code Project

Bible Code Project Documentation for Developers
If you'd like to try your hand at developing your own Bible website, the Bible Code Project is a collection of GitHub repositories containing source code and data that anyone can use to develop their own computerized Bible project. All of the data and source code for each of these projects is in the public domain and free of copyrights, therefore it's free for you to use. If you use or change any of the source code or data files in the Bible Code Project for your own ministry or Bible project, please change the name of your project, so people don't get confused.
