New Christian Bible - Media

This is a list of movies, shows, music, and testimonials about our savior Jesus Christ that I've found to be very inspiring. The quality of these productions is excellent, and I believe they're inspired by the Holy Spirit. I believe these productions were given to us by God to use in our ministries to help teach those who hunger for God's word. My favorite is "The Chosen", it has presented a new perspective on Jesus that I always knew was there, but could never find the right words to convey this new outlook to others. Most of the movies, music, and shows listed here are supported by advertising, but "The Bible Project" receives no money for these recommendations, they're just things that I like.

Movies

  1. Acts: The Acts of the Apostles
    1994 • 3 hr 12 min • TV-G • Drama
    "The Book of Acts of the Apostles" is a faithful re-enactment of the joys, trials, tribulations, and miracles of the early days of the Christian Church as they struggled to spread their message of salvation through Jesus Christ.

  2. Jesus Revolution
    2023 • 1 hr 59 min • PG-13 • Drama
    A hippie street preacher, an unhappy teen and a straitlaced California pastor wind up creating a new kind of church after crossing paths in the '70s. This movie is excellent, but it's not available to stream for free. It is however available to stream for a fee on all these services; Amazon Prime, Apple TV, Google Play Movies, Netflix, VUDU, and YouTube. I like this movie because it's the only one I've ever seen that tells the story of how I met Jesus.

  3. Joshua
    2002 • 1 hr 30 min • G • Drama
    A kind unknown carpenter arrives in the rural town of Auburn and begins repairing a small Baptist church that was destroyed in a storm the year before. When they see him performing miracles, the whole town is shaken to it's core when they realize that he may be the risen Christ.

  4. Paul the Apostle
    2013 • 2 hr 24 min • TV-PG • Drama
    Paul the Apostle travels throughout the Gentile world delivering the good news of the salvation that Jesus Christ brought to the world. Paul relays the story of God's sacrifice of His only son to save the world from it's sin. This story was beautifully filmed in the Moroccan desert.

  5. The Christmas Candle
    2013 • 1 hr 40 min • PG • Drama
    A pastor, who has suffered a personal loss, and is struggling with a loss of faith, accepts a position in a small town that believes in a miraculous candle that's delivered every Christmas by an angel, which is to be given to one person in need. He believes the practice to be on the verge of blasphemy, and he strives to abolish the practice.

  6. The Encounter
    2010 • TV-PG • 1 hr 30 min • Drama
    After a road is closed, five strangers find themselves involuntarily introduced to each other at an abandoned roadside diner where they experience a supernatural encounter with the cook ... Jesus Christ.

  7. The Encounter - Paradise Lost
    2012 • 1 hr 49 min • TV-14 • Drama
    Six strangers find themselves facing a hurricane as they are trapped in a beach hotel, seven years after the most devastating tsunami in Thailand. Jesus shows up to offer each one of them some life changing advice.

  8. The Life of Jesus
    2003 • PG-13 • 3 hr 3 min • Drama
    A re-enactment of the life of Jesus as testified to in the Gospels by John the Apostle.

  9. The Time Changer
    2002 • 1 hr 37 min • PG • Drama
    In 1890 a Bible scholar and theologian is convinced that his colleague has lost his senses when his friend tries to convince him to embark on a journey in the time machine his father built. Thinking he will prove his friend to be delusional, he climbs aboard the machine. He's stunned to find himself in the 21st century, and shocked to see what detrimental effects a book he wrote will have on future generations.

Series and Shows

  1. A.D. The Bible Continues
    A series of movies based on the stories in the Bible.
    2015 • Season 1

    1. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Tomb is Open
      2015 • 44 min • S1:E1
      The Jewish High Priest Caiaphas and Roman Governor Pontius Pilate conspire to crucify Jesus in order to placate the population and end the chaos in Jerusalem. Peter and the disciples are terrified and go into hiding after the crucifixion, it is their darkest hour.

    2. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Body is Gone
      2015 • 43m • S1 E2
      Pilate and Caiaphas believe that Jesus' body has been stolen from the tomb. They believe the disciples have done this in an effort to convince the masses that he has risen from the dead in a plot to take power. They don't believe that he's come back to life, and the disciples flee Gallilee as Pilate and Caiaphas frantically search for the followers of Jesus.

    3. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Spirit Arrives
      2015 • 43m • S1:E3
      During the festival of Pentecost, zealot revolutionaries try to assassinate Pilate. The Holy Spirit descends upon the Virgin Mary and the disciples, and they start to preach the Gospel. Caiaphas has Peter and John arrested, but fails to have them convicted.

    4. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Wrath
      2015 • 44m • S1:E4
      When a Roman soldier is murdered Pilate responds with a mass of crucifixions of Jesus Christ's followers. Caiaphas still can't convict John and Peter of heresey and blasphemy.

    5. A.D. The Bible Continues - The First Martyr
      2015 • 44m • S1:E5
      Caiaphas fails to silence the disciples, and Stephen speaks out as he preaches and testifies about the risen Christ. He's arrested, put on trial, and crucified for his refusal to be silenced by the High Priests.

    6. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Persecution
      2015 • 44m • S1:E6
      Caiaphas fails to silence the disciples, and his position of High Priest is challenged. In response he authorizes Saul of Tarsus to begin a campaign of horrofic persecution against Jesus' followers.

    7. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Visit
      2015 • 43m • S1:E7
      Saul targets Peter as his persecution intensifies against the disciples and followers of Jesus. The Roman Emperor arrives in Jerusalem to decide Pilate's fate when he receives reports of the chaos there. Many faithful followers flee the city, spreading God's word as they go.

    8. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Road to Damascus
      2015 • 43m • S1:E8
      As Saul continues to hunt Peter, he has a life changing event when Jesus appears to him in a vision and he's blinded on the road to Damascus. Pontius Pilate's wife Claudia has nightmares, and Pilate's fate takes a turn for the worse.

    9. A.D. The Bible Continues - Saul's Return
      2015 • 43m • S1:E9
      After his trip to Damascus, Saul returns to Jerusalem a changed man. When Tiberius' evil nephew Caligula strangles him to death, the newly coronated Emperor demands that his statue be setup in the temple. Pilate and Caiaphas knew that the Jews would consider it to be a desecration, and it would cause a rebellion amongst the people.

    10. A.D. The Bible Continues - Brothers in Arms
      2015 • 44m • S1:E10
      When Saul begins to preach in Jerusalem Caiaphas is enraged. James the Just comes to help Peter defend himself from Caiaphas. The Zealots and Ethiopians conspire together to plot a revolt against the Roman Empire.

    11. A.D. The Bible Continues - Rise Up
      2015 • 44m • S1:E11
      James confronts Caiaphas and wins a reprieve for the disciples. The army of Zealots grows but their Ethiopian supporter turns to Jesus. When Peter demonstrates God's power through faith through Tabitha's resurrection many people are drawn to His message.

    12. A.D. The Bible Continues - The Abomination
      2015 • 44m • S1:E12
      Emperor Caligula's statue arrives in Jerusalem, and the Jewish population storms the temple in protest. The disciples continue to spread Jesus' message, and Cornelius becomes the first Roman citizen convert.

Pharoah refuses, Egypt must suffer ten devastating plagues before he sets them free, After the Red Sea is parted, Moses leads them to freedom. He tells them to wait there for the Holy Spirit to come before they go out into the world to continue his ministry and build his church.
