`<div class="cs-ncbPage"><h4 id="ncbsf">New Christian Bible Statement of Faith</h4><hr><p>These are The Things I Believe<br><br><p>I. I believe there is only one God, who is, was, and always will be, and I believe that God is the Holy Trinity: Father, Son and the Holy Spirit.</p><p>II. I believe that Jesus said, 'If you have seen me you have seen the Father.'</p><p>III. I believe that Jesus Christ was immacuately conceived by the Holy Spirit and was born of a virgin named Mary in a little town named Bethlehem. <p>IV. I believe that Jesus is the only begotten son of God, and that he came to earth not to judge the world, but to save the world.</p><p>V. I believe that Jesus Christ is the savior of the world.</p><p>VI. I believe that Jesus Christ lived, was crucified, died, and rose back to life on the third day after he was buried.</p><p>VII. I believe that Jesus said, 'I am the Way, the Truth, and the Life.'</p><p>VIII. I believe that Jesus said, 'The time has been fulfilled, and the kingdom of God is at hand; repent and believe in the gospel.' <p>IX. I believe that Jesus said, 'Truly, I say to you, there are some standing here who will not taste death until they see the kingdom of God after it has come with power.'</p><p>X. I believe that Jesus said, 'Truly, I say to you, unless one is born again he cannot see the kingdom of God.'</p><p>XI. I believe that the Kingdom of Heaven is here, now because I have seen it.</p><br><p align="center"><em>God loves you, Jesus loves you, I love you, and this is God's free gift to you .....</em></p></div>`
